Employee Handbook - Remote Work Policy

Remote work is permitted for all eligible employees with manager approval. Employees must maintain regular communication and meet all productivity standards while working from home.

Key Requirements:
1. Maintain regular work hours
2. Use company communication tools
3. Attend virtual meetings as scheduled
4. Complete all assigned tasks on time
5. Report any technical issues immediately

Benefits of Remote Work:
- Improved work-life balance
- Reduced commute time
- Increased productivity
- Better focus on tasks
- Flexible scheduling options

This policy applies to all full-time employees who have been with the company for at least 6 months and have demonstrated reliable performance.
